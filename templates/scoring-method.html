Scoring Methodology

Learn More about Our Algorithms in this Comprehensive Write-Up. To build the machine learning models, I leveraged Apple's open-source machine learning library, Turi Create, as it's incredibly flexible to develop custom recommendation models.

Trail Secrets has five different recommendation algorithms that provide insight into Hiking Trails depending on what you're looking for.

  • Item Content: "If you like the Mount Si Trail, here are hikes that have the most similar hiking attributes to the Mount Si Trail."
  • Rank Factorization. Personalized recommendations based on how you've rated hikes on AllTrails. "For Perry, an AllTrails user that has rated some hikes, here are the hikes that Perry would likely rate very highly."
  • Power Ratings: A custom blended scoring algorithm that takes into account the # of reviews and the user reviews for a given hike
  • Popularity based on # of Reviews
  • Popularity based on Review Ratings (Must Have at least 100 Reviews)
